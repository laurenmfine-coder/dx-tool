⚠️ Other Causes to Consider

  • Anastomotic leak: POD 5-7, abdominal surgery, peritonitis
  • C. diff colitis: Any time with antibiotics
  • Transfusion reaction: During/after transfusion
  • Malignant hyperthermia: Intraop with volatile anesthetics
