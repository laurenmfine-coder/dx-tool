High-yield comparison tables, diagnostic algorithms, and visual summaries — all sourced from open-access evidence

How to Use QuickRef

These cards give you the high-yield facts in visual format — comparison tables, diagnostic flowcharts, and clinical pearls. They're designed for rapid review, not comprehensive teaching. Each card links directly to the full StatPearls article for deeper learning.

Pair with: Use QuickRef alongside CRTs (for clinical reasoning practice), Adventures (for case-based learning), and the Quiz Bank (for self-testing).

💡 All reference content links to StatPearls — a free, peer-reviewed, continuously updated medical education resource. We don't try to replace it; we help you reason through it.
