🧠 Memory Aid: Catalase + vs −

Catalase + = Staph + Listeria (staphylococci are CATalase positive — cats have staff)
Catalase − = Strep + Enterococcus

🧠 Memory Aid: α vs β Hemolysis

α (partial/green) = S. pneumoniae, Viridans strep (viridans = "green" in Latin)
β (complete/clear) = S. pyogenes (GAS), S. agalactiae (GBS), S. aureus, Listeria

💎 Board Pearl: Optochin differentiates the two α-hemolytic strep: S. pneumoniae is Optochin Sensitive (pneumo = Sensitive), Viridans is Optochin Resistant.
💎 Board Pearl: Quellung reaction = capsular swelling with antibody. Most heavily encapsulated organisms: SHiNE SKiS — Salmonella, H. influenzae, Neisseria, E. coli, Strep pneumo, Klebsiella, Group B Strep.
