My Approach to Acid-Base Problems

1. Look at pH (acidemia vs alkalemia) 2. Identify primary disorder (CO2 or HCO3) 3. Check compensation (Winter's formula for met acidosis) 4. Calculate anion gap if met acidosis 5. Calculate delta-delta if AG elevated
