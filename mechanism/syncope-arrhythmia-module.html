Syncope & Arrhythmias

Cardiac conduction, syncope mechanisms, AFib, and ventricular arrhythmias

⚠️ High-Risk Syncope Features

Exertional syncope
Syncope while supine/sitting
Associated chest pain
Family history sudden death
Abnormal ECG findings

✓ Low-Risk Features

Prodrome (warmth, nausea)
Standing for long period
After painful stimulus
Young, no cardiac history
