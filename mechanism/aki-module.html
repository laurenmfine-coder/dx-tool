Acute Kidney Injury

Pre-renal, intrinsic, and post-renal mechanisms with urinalysis interpretation

📚 20 Questions
⏱️ ~45 minutes

📊 AKI Categories

Pre-Renal (55-60%)
Hypovolemia, CHF, Cirrhosis, NSAIDs, ACEi/ARB
Intrinsic (35-40%)
ATN, AIN, Glomerulonephritis, Vascular
Post-Renal (5-10%)
BPH, Stones, Malignancy, Stricture

🔬 Key Lab Findings

Pre-Renal
FENa <1%, BUN:Cr >20:1, UOsm >500
ATN
FENa >2%, BUN:Cr ~10:1, Muddy casts
AIN
WBC casts, Eosinophiluria, Sterile pyuria
