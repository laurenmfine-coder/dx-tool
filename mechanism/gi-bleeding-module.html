📊 Upper GIB Causes

Peptic ulcer disease35-50%
Erosive disease20-30%
Varices10-20%
Mallory-Weiss tear5-10%

📊 Lower GIB Causes

Diverticulosis30-40%
Hemorrhoids10-20%

Angiodysplasia5-10%
