Our Educational Philosophy

MechanismDx doesn't replace your study resources—we provide the reasoning layer that makes them work better. Learn the facts from First Aid, Sketchy, and Pathoma. Understand mechanisms with Osmosis. Practice questions on UWorld and AMBOSS. Then come to MechanismDx to test whether you can predict, reason, and calibrate your knowledge.

"Learn the facts anywhere. Learn to think here."
