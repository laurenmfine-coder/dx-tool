⚙️ Rheumatologic Emergencies

Rheumatology

🔬 Pathophysiology

Autoimmune Crisis

Acute manifestations of rheumatic diseases.

Underlying autoimmune disease→Flare or complication→Organ-threatening involvement→Rapid assessment needed→Immunosuppression + supportive care

🏥 Clinical Correlations

Septic arthritis

Mechanism: Hot swollen joint - infection until proven otherwise

Scleroderma renal crisis

Mechanism: Hypertensive emergency with AKI - treat with ACE-I

🔑 Key Points

✓ Septic arthritis: aspirate joint, empiric antibiotics
✓ Scleroderma renal crisis: ACE inhibitors (not contraindicated!)
✓ Lupus flare: high-dose steroids for major organs
✓ Vasculitis: may need cyclophosphamide
