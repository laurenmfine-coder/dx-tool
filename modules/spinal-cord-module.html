⚙️ Spinal Cord Compression

Neurology

🔬 Pathophysiology

Cord Compression

Mass or lesion compresses spinal cord causing dysfunction.

Mass effect (tumor, abscess, hematoma)→Cord compression→Vascular compromise→Edema and ischemia→Neurologic deficits

🏥 Clinical Correlations

Back pain + weakness + bladder dysfunction

Mechanism: Classic triad of cord compression

Sensory level

Mechanism: Dermatomal cutoff indicates lesion location

🔑 Key Points

✓ Triad: pain + weakness + bladder/bowel
✓ MRI entire spine urgently
✓ Dexamethasone immediately if suspected
✓ Malignancy is common cause in adults
✓ Time is function - delays worsen outcomes
