TREATMENT
Management

Hepatitis B

Acute: Supportive (95% of adults clear spontaneously). Chronic: Treat if active replication (elevated HBV DNA, elevated ALT, fibrosis). Tenofovir or entecavir (nucleos(t)ide analogs — first-line). PEG-interferon alternative. Screen for HCC every 6 months.

Hepatitis C

DAAs (direct-acting antivirals): sofosbuvir-based regimens (sofosbuvir/velpatasvir = pan-genotypic). 8-12 weeks. SVR >95%. Screen all adults 18-79 at least once. Treat ALL diagnosed patients regardless of fibrosis stage.

Prevention

HBV vaccine: 3-dose series. All infants + high-risk adults. HBIG + vaccine for infants born to HBsAg+ mothers (within 12h). HAV vaccine: 2-dose series. Travelers, chronic liver disease, MSM, IVDU.
