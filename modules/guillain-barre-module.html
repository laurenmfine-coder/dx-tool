MECHANISM
Pathophysiology

GBS = acute inflammatory demyelinating polyradiculoneuropathy (AIDP). Autoimmune attack on peripheral nerve myelin triggered by preceding infection (1-4 weeks prior).

TriggerDetails
Campylobacter jejuni#1 trigger (~30%). Associated with axonal variants and anti-ganglioside antibodies (anti-GM1)
CMV, EBV, MycoplasmaOther common preceding infections
Influenza vaccineHistorical association (1976 swine flu). Current risk is extremely low
🔑 Ascending Weakness Pattern: Starts distally in legs → ascends proximally → may involve arms → cranial nerves → respiratory muscles. Areflexia/hyporeflexia is hallmark. Bilateral and relatively symmetric. Peak severity at 2-4 weeks → plateau → gradual recovery.
CLINICAL
Diagnosis
⚠️ Respiratory Failure: 30% of GBS patients require mechanical ventilation. Monitor FVC (forced vital capacity) q4-6h. Intubate if FVC <20 mL/kg or NIF <-30 cmH2O (20/30 rule). Do NOT wait for hypoxia — it's a late sign.

Diagnostic Studies

TestFindings
CSF (LP)Albuminocytologic dissociation: elevated protein with NORMAL cell count. Classic finding (may be normal in first week)
NCS/EMGDemyelinating pattern: prolonged distal latencies, conduction block, slowed conduction velocities
ClinicalProgressive bilateral ascending weakness + areflexia + preceding infection 1-4 weeks prior
🔑 GBS vs Myasthenia Gravis: GBS = ascending, areflexia, sensory involvement, CSF protein elevated. MG = descending (bulbar → limbs), fatigable weakness, normal reflexes, NO sensory loss, anti-AChR antibodies.
TREATMENT
Management
TreatmentDetails
IVIGFirst-line. 0.4 g/kg/day × 5 days. Equal efficacy to plasmapheresis
Plasmapheresis (PLEX)Alternative first-line. 5 exchanges over 2 weeks. Removes circulating antibodies
SteroidsNOT effective in GBS (unlike CIDP)
SupportiveICU monitoring, respiratory support, DVT prophylaxis, pain management, PT/OT
🔑 Do NOT combine IVIG + PLEX (IVIG would be removed by plasmapheresis). Choose one. Do NOT give corticosteroids — ineffective and may worsen outcomes. Recovery: ~80% walk independently at 1 year.
