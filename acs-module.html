Myocardial O₂ Supply vs Demand

O₂ Supply

  • • Coronary blood flow (thrombus occludes this)
  • • O₂ content (anemia worsens ischemia)
  • • Diastolic time (tachycardia reduces)

O₂ Demand

  • • Heart rate (major determinant)
  • • Wall tension (preload × afterload)
  • • Contractility (sympathetic drive)
Why Beta-blockers work: ↓HR, ↓BP, ↓Contractility = ↓O₂ demand

STEMI: Time is Muscle

  • • Door-to-balloon ≤90 min
  • • Primary PCI preferred
  • • Fibrinolytics if PCI unavailable

NSTE-ACS: Risk Stratify

  • • Early invasive (24-72hr) if high-risk
  • • TIMI score guides timing
  • • Conservative if low-risk
