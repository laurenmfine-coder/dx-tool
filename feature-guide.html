🔄 The 5-Stage Workflow

Each case follows a structured diagnostic workflow that mirrors real clinical reasoning:

  • 1
    Door Note — Read the chief complaint, form initial impressions
  • 2
    Differential — Generate your initial differential diagnosis
  • 3
    History — Ask questions to the AI patient chatbot
  • 4
    Physical Exam — Select targeted exam maneuvers
  • 5
    Workup — Order tests and refine your diagnosis
3 / 4
📊

📊 Confidence Calibration

At three key points, you'll rate your confidence from 0-100%. This helps you understand how well-calibrated your certainty is.

  • 📈
    After Door Note — How confident are you in your initial impression?
  • 📈
    After History — Did talking to the patient change your thinking?
  • 📈
    After Workup — How sure are you of your final diagnosis?

Goal: Your confidence should match your accuracy. If you're 80% confident, you should be right ~80% of the time.

🧠 Cognitive Bias Feedback

ReasonDx watches for common diagnostic errors and provides real-time feedback on your reasoning patterns:

  • ⚠️
    Anchoring — Are you stuck on your first impression?
  • ⚠️
    Premature Closure — Did you stop searching too soon?
  • ⚠️
    Confirmation Bias — Are you only seeking supporting evidence?
  • ⚠️
    Availability — Is a recent case skewing your judgment?
