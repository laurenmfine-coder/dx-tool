💡 Clinical Decision Pearls

High Eosinophils (≥300): Anti-IL-5 agents (mepolizumab, benralizumab) or dupilumab are excellent choices. Benralizumab causes near-complete eosinophil depletion and has q8wk maintenance dosing.
Allergic Asthma with High IgE: Omalizumab is first-line if IgE 30-1500 IU/mL and positive aeroallergen sensitization. Longest safety track record (20+ years).
Low/Unknown Eosinophils: Tezepelumab works across phenotypes including low-eos asthma. Consider when T2 biomarkers are low or phenotype unclear.
Multiple Atopic Comorbidities: Dupilumab covers asthma + atopic dermatitis + CRSwNP + EoE. One drug for multiple conditions = simplified regimen.
OCS-Dependent Asthma: All asthma biologics can reduce OCS use. Anti-IL-5 agents and dupilumab have strong OCS-sparing data. Goal is complete OCS elimination.
EGPA (Churg-Strauss): Mepolizumab is the only biologic FDA-approved for EGPA. Reduces relapse and allows OCS tapering.
Food Allergy: Omalizumab is FDA-approved to reduce allergic reactions to accidental food exposure in patients 1+ years old. Does NOT replace food avoidance or epinephrine for anaphylaxis.
