Antifungal Agents

Targeting Ergosterol & the Fungal Cell Wall

🎯 Antifungal Targets

Fungal cells have ERGOSTEROL in their membranes (humans have cholesterol). This is the key selective toxicity target.

  • Azoles: Block ergosterol SYNTHESIS (inhibit lanosterol 14α-demethylase)
  • Amphotericin B: BINDS directly to ergosterol → membrane pores
  • Echinocandins: Inhibit β-glucan cell WALL synthesis
  • Terbinafine: Blocks squalene epoxidase → ↓ ergosterol synthesis

🔵 AZOLES - Ergosterol Synthesis Inhibitors

Mechanism: Inhibit lanosterol 14α-demethylase (CYP51) → ↓ ergosterol

🟣 AMPHOTERICIN B - Ergosterol Binder

Mechanism: Binds ergosterol → membrane pores → cell death (FUNGICIDAL)

  • Broadest spectrum of any antifungal
  • Lipid formulations preferred (less nephrotoxicity)
  • Uses: Mucormycosis (DOC), Cryptococcal meningitis (+ flucytosine), severe endemic mycoses

⚠️ "Ampho-terrible" Toxicities

  • Nephrotoxicity: Dose-limiting! Prehydrate with NS.
  • Infusion reactions: "Shake and bake" - fever, chills, rigors
  • Electrolyte wasting: Hypokalemia, hypomagnesemia

🟢 ECHINOCANDINS - Cell Wall Inhibitors

Mechanism: Inhibit β(1,3)-D-glucan synthase → disrupt cell wall

  • Drugs: Caspofungin, micafungin, anidulafungin (IV only)
  • DOC for invasive candidiasis (including resistant species)
  • Very well tolerated
  • NO activity: Cryptococcus, Mucormycosis, endemic fungi
Echinocandins DON'T work against Cryptococcus (lacks significant β-glucan in cell wall)!

💊 Other Antifungals

  • Flucytosine: Always with AmB for cryptococcal meningitis. Marrow toxicity.
  • Terbinafine: Squalene epoxidase inhibitor. DOC for onychomycosis. Concentrates in keratin.
  • Nystatin: Topical only (like AmB but too toxic for systemic). Thrush treatment.
  • Griseofulvin: Dermatophytes only. Largely replaced by terbinafine.

📝 High-Yield Summary

  • Azoles: Inhibit 14α-demethylase → ↓ ergosterol. CYP inhibitors! Voriconazole = Aspergillus DOC.
  • Amphotericin B: Binds ergosterol → pores. Broadest spectrum. NEPHROTOXIC.
  • Echinocandins: β-glucan synthesis inhibitors. DOC for candidemia. NO Crypto/Mucor!
  • Flucytosine: Only with AmB for crypto meningitis.
  • Terbinafine: DOC for onychomycosis.
  • Mucormycosis: AmB or isavuconazole (vori doesn't work!)
