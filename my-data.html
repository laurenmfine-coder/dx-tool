    Data Protection

    How your data is protected:
    • Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage
    • Before any content update, an automatic backup is created
    • If content changes (modules renamed, quiz bank updated), your progress migrates automatically
    • You can export your data anytime as a portable JSON file
    • Importing restores your progress on any device with ReasonDx
