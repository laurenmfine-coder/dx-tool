📝 H&P Template

CC: Chief complaint in patient's words
HPI: Onset, duration, character, severity, associated sx
PMH: Medical history, surgical history
Medications: Current meds with doses
Allergies: Drug allergies and reactions
Social Hx: Tobacco, alcohol, drugs, occupation
Family Hx: Relevant family history
ROS: Review of systems by organ
Physical Exam: Vitals, general, HEENT, CV, Pulm, Abd, Ext, Neuro
Labs/Studies: Results and interpretation
Assessment: Problem list with reasoning
Plan: By problem, specific orders

⚡ Quick Tips

  • Lead with the diagnosis in your assessment
  • Be specific in your plan (doses, frequencies)
  • Include contingency plans ("if...then...")
  • Document your clinical reasoning
