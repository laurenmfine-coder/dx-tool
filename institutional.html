The Problem We're Solving

Your students arrive on the wards
before their reasoning is ready

Clinical reasoning is the most consequential skill in medicine — and the hardest to teach at scale. Students memorize facts but can't synthesize them under pressure. Clerkship directors see it. Residency programs feel it. LCME and ACGME are measuring it. ReasonDx gives you the infrastructure to develop and document it.

46%
of diagnostic errors involve cognitive failures in clinical reasoning — not knowledge gaps (AHRQ, 2022)
LCME ED-6
requires documentation of clinical reasoning skill development and formative assessment across the curriculum
EPA 1
"Gather a history and perform physical exam" — the first EPA assumes reasoning readiness most programs can't verify

Most programs are fully deployed within 5–10 business days of contract signing. This includes LMS integration, SSO setup, faculty account provisioning, and roster import. We handle the technical setup — your IT team just needs to provide SSO credentials and an LMS API key.

All student data is institution-scoped — meaning only faculty and administrators at your institution can see your students' data. We do not sell or use student data for advertising or model training.

ReasonDx is complementary, not competitive, to question banks and traditional case libraries. Question banks build knowledge recall; ReasonDx builds reasoning process.
