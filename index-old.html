THE PLATFORM

Three layers. One ecosystem.

ReasonDx is both the name of the platform and one of three integrated learning layers. Each layer makes the others stronger — practicing cases surfaces gaps in mechanism knowledge, understanding mechanisms improves reasoning, and coaching reveals the blind spots you can't see yourself.

🧠
Layer 1 — Reason
ReasonDx
Build differentials, take histories from AI patients, order workups, interpret results, and make disposition decisions. 326 cases across 29 specialties with progressive disclosure and AI feedback.
→
⚙️
Layer 2 — Understand
MechanismDx
When you miss a case, understand why. Pathophysiology modules explain the mechanism behind each diagnosis — anatomy, pharmacology, and the clinical reasoning chain that connects symptoms to disease.
→
🗣️
Layer 3 — Reflect
CoachDx
An AI clinical coach uses Socratic questioning to surface reasoning patterns, cognitive biases, and growth areas. No spoilers — it guides you to the answer yourself, the way an attending teaches at the bedside.
🔄

The reinforcing loop

Practice a case in ReasonDx → review the mechanism in MechanismDx → debrief your reasoning with CoachDx → return to the next case with sharper instincts. Each rotation through the loop compounds your clinical judgment.
