Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

ReasonDx ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account InformationName, email address, training level, institution (optional), specialty interest (optional)Account creation and personalization
Usage DataCases completed, quiz scores, time spent, feature interactionsProgress tracking and platform improvement
Payment DataProcessed by Stripe — we never see or store your full card numberSubscription billing

What we do NOT collect: We do not collect patient data, clinical records, social security numbers, or any health information about real patients. ReasonDx is an educational platform — all clinical scenarios are fictional.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and maintain the Platform; track your learning progress and display personalized content; process payments for subscription plans; communicate important updates about the Platform; and improve the educational content and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Account and progress data is stored using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure) with encryption at rest and in transit. Payment data is processed and stored solely by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our users.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your account and associated data; export your learning progress data; and opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., payment records for tax compliance).

7. Cookies and Local Storage

ReasonDx uses browser localStorage to maintain your login session and save your learning preferences (e.g., theme selection, progress data). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising. Analytics are used solely to improve the Platform experience.

8. Children's Privacy

ReasonDx is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the Platform or email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion. We do not sell personal information. For CCPA requests, contact us at the email below.
