🔑 Board Pearl: Surfactant is produced by type II pneumocytes starting at ~24 weeks, adequate levels by ~35 weeks. Lecithin:sphingomyelin (L:S) ratio ≥2 = lung maturity. Betamethasone given to mother 24-34 weeks to accelerate maturity.
