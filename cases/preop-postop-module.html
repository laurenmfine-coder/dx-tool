MECHANISM
Preoperative Assessment

Cardiac Risk — RCRI (Lee Index)

For non-cardiac surgery: high-risk surgery, ischemic heart disease, CHF, cerebrovascular disease, insulin-dependent DM, Cr >2. Each = 1 point. ≥3 = high risk.

🔑 Perioperative Beta-Blockers: Continue if already taking. Do NOT start de novo (POISE trial: reduced MI but increased stroke/mortality).

Medication Management

ContinueHold
Beta-blockers, statins, anti-epileptics, thyroid meds, inhalersWarfarin (5 days), DOACs (2-3 days), clopidogrel (5-7 days), metformin (day of surgery), ACEi/ARBs (hypotension risk), herbals

NPO Guidelines

Clear liquids: 2h. Breast milk: 4h. Light meal: 6h. Full meal: 8h. "2-4-6-8, don't aspirate."

CLINICAL
Post-Op Complications by Timeline
⚠️ Post-Op Fever (5 W's):
POD 0-1 Wind: Atelectasis (#1 early fever). Incentive spirometry
POD 3-5 Water: UTI (especially with Foley), pneumonia
POD 5-7 Wound: Surgical site infection
POD 5-7 Walk: DVT/PE
POD 7+ Wonder drugs: Drug fever, C. diff, abscess

Other Complications

ComplicationKey Points
IleusExpected 3-5 days after abdominal surgery. NPO, NG tube, ambulate, minimize opioids
Anastomotic leakPOD 5-7. Fever + tachycardia + peritonitis. CT confirms. → OR
Adrenal crisisChronic steroid patients → stress-dose hydrocortisone 100 mg IV
TREATMENT
Fluids & DVT Prophylaxis

Fluid Therapy

Maintenance: 4-2-1 rule. Resuscitation: LR preferred over NS. Transfusion: Hb <7 (restrictive strategy).

VTE Prophylaxis

RiskProphylaxis
LowEarly ambulation
ModerateLMWH or UFH + SCDs
High (ortho, cancer)LMWH/DOAC + mechanical. Extended 28-35 days post hip/knee/cancer surgery
🔑 Abx Prophylaxis: Within 60 min of incision (120 for vanc). Cefazolin for most clean cases. Cefazolin + metronidazole for bowel surgery. Discontinue within 24h post-op.
