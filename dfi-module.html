⚙️ Diabetic Foot Infection

Infectious Disease

🔬 Pathophysiology

Neuropathy → Ulcer → Infection

Peripheral neuropathy leads to unrecognized trauma and ulceration.

Diabetic neuropathy→Loss of protective sensation→Unrecognized trauma→Ulcer formation→Bacterial infection + spread

🏥 Clinical Correlations

Ulcer with purulent drainage

Mechanism: Infection of soft tissue

Probe-to-bone positive

Mechanism: High specificity for osteomyelitis

🔑 Key Points

✓ PEDIS classification for severity
✓ Probe-to-bone suggests osteomyelitis
✓ Polymicrobial in chronic/severe
✓ MRI for osteomyelitis diagnosis
✓ Broad-spectrum antibiotics + wound care
