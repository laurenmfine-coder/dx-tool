Your Clinical Reasoning Fingerprint

Every learner has a unique pattern of strengths and gaps. The Dx Suite tracks your performance across all three platforms to build a comprehensive picture of your diagnostic reasoning abilities.

Unlike simple percentage scores, your fingerprint shows how you think—which steps of the diagnostic process come naturally and which need more work.

  • 📊
    Cross-Platform Tracking
    Data from cases, quizzes, and coaching sessions all contribute
  • 🎯

  • 📈

Maximize Your Learning

Recommended Learning Paths

Different approaches for different learning goals.

🎯

Deep Dive

Master one topic completely before moving on. Best for dedicated study sessions.

  1. 1 Complete a ReasonDx case
  2. 2 Study the linked MechanismDx module
  3. 3 Discuss with Dr. Chen in CoachDx
  4. 4 Try the parallel case
⚡

Quick Practice

Short, focused sessions when time is limited. Great for daily practice.

  1. 1 Do one CoachDx session (15 min)
  2. 2 Complete one MechanismDx quiz
  3. 3 Review your fingerprint
  4. 4 Note gaps for later deep dives
🔍

Gap-Focused

Target your weaknesses directly based on your fingerprint analysis.

  1. 1 Check your reasoning fingerprint
  2. 2 Go to recommended MechanismDx module
  3. 3 Practice linked ReasonDx cases
  4. 4 Re-check fingerprint for improvement
