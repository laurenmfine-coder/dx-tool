These tools are designed to help medical students develop clinical reasoning skills beyond traditional didactic learning.

After using these tools, you will be able to:

  • Prioritization of differential diagnoses
  • Focused vs. comprehensive history taking
  • Targeted physical examination
  • OSCE preparation
