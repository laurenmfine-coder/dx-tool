The Learning Arc
How a Session Works

Start anywhere. The system meets you there.

1
Pick a topic or let the system choose

Search for a diagnosis, browse the Case Library, or ask CoachDx to target your weakest area. No rigid curriculum — you lead.

2
Work through it in one or all three modes

Coach → Case → Mechanism in any order. Hit a gap and the system surfaces a bridge. Your session context travels with you.

3
Your gaps become your next session

Every bridge taken, every hesitation logged — built into your Reasoning Fingerprint and returned through spaced repetition until mastered.
