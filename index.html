🩺 Encounter Log — Preview
SETTING
Cardiology Clinic · Dr. Patel
WHAT I OBSERVED
Patient came in for follow-up after stent placement. Doctor listened to heart and lungs, checked ankle swelling. Adjusted medication because BP was still high.
MY QUESTION
Why did they check the ankles? What does swelling there mean?
AI ANALYSIS
The doctor was checking for peripheral edema — fluid buildup in the lower extremities. After stent placement, this can indicate the heart isn't pumping efficiently (heart failure), or it could be a medication side effect (calcium channel blockers like amlodipine commonly cause ankle swelling)...

🎯 Diagnostic Challenge
BEGINNER

A 20-year-old college student comes to the clinic because she's been feeling really tired for the past 3 weeks. She says she's been sleeping more but still feels exhausted. She's also noticed she's been craving ice chips and her nails look different.

💡 REASONING SCAFFOLD

When a young person is tired, there are many causes. But craving ice (called pica) and nail changes are very specific clues. What common condition causes all three?

📚 VOCABULARY HELP

Pica: Craving non-food items (ice, dirt, starch) — often a sign of iron deficiency.
Koilonychia: Spoon-shaped nails — another classic iron deficiency sign.
