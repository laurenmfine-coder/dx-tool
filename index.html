Questions
Frequently asked
Is ReasonDx free? +
The free tier gives you full access to CaseDx cases, the Virtual EMR, and MechanismDx topics. CoachDx AI coaching, Night Float, spaced repetition, and advanced analytics unlock with Pro ($14.99/month or $99/year).
Who built ReasonDx? +
ReasonDx was built by Lauren Fine, MD, FAAAAI, a board-certified allergist/immunologist and medical educator with faculty appointments in clinical skills education. Dr. Fine is a recognized innovator in clinical reasoning curriculum design and health professions education.
How is CoachDx different from just using ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT gives you answers. CoachDx asks you questions. It's specifically designed for clinical reasoning education using Socratic questioning — it probes your thinking, pushes back on vague answers, and builds sessions around your documented gaps. It won't tell you the diagnosis; it will ask you to defend every step until your reasoning is airtight.
Is there evidence this approach works? +
ReasonDx is built on three approaches with robust empirical support in health professions education: deliberate practice (Ericsson), spaced repetition (Ebbinghaus), and Socratic coaching. These principles underpin the design of every case, coaching session, and mechanism module on the platform.
Can my institution use ReasonDx? +
Yes. ReasonDx offers institutional licensing with faculty dashboards, cohort analytics, LMS integration (Canvas/Blackboard), and ACGME milestone tracking. Learn more about institutional access or email hello@reasondx.com.
Does it work on mobile? +
MechanismDx, CoachDx, and most CaseDx cases work well on mobile. For the full ED Track Board and split-screen EMR experience, a laptop or tablet gives you the most screen real estate.
Using ReasonDx in your program?

Faculty dashboards, cohort analytics, Canvas/Blackboard integration, and ACGME milestone mapping. Designed for medical schools, PA programs, NP programs, and residencies.
