⚙️ ACLS Rhythms

Pathophysiology | Cardiology

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Shockable: VF, pVT
  • Non-shockable: asystole, PEA
  • Hs and Ts for reversible causes
  • Epi q3-5min, amio after 3rd shock

🔬 Core Pathophysiology

Shockable Rhythms

VF and pVT respond to defibrillation.

Pathway

Identify VF/pVT→Immediate shock→CPR 2 min→Rhythm check→Repeat if needed

Non-Shockable

Asystole/PEA need CPR and cause treatment.

Pathway

Identify asystole/PEA→CPR + epi→Find Hs and Ts→Continue until ROSC

🏥 Clinical Correlations

VF (chaotic)

Mechanism: Disorganized ventricular activity

Significance: Shockable

PEA (organized, no pulse)

Mechanism: Electrical without mechanical

Significance: Non-shockable, treat cause
