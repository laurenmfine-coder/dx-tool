I thought it was PE because of the dyspnea and tachycardia. Why was ACS the better answer?
Great question! Both can present with dyspnea and tachycardia. The key differentiators here were:

• Substernal pressure – classic for ACS, uncommon in PE
• Radiation to left arm – highly specific for cardiac ischemia
• Exertional onset – suggests increased myocardial demand
