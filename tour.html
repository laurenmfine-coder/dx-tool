💡 About the naming

The Dx Ecosystem (at reasondx.com) contains three integrated platforms: ReasonDx for clinical cases, MechanismDx for pathophysiology, and CoachDx for AI coaching.

🩺 Platform 1 of 3

ReasonDx

Practice clinical decision-making with interactive patient cases that challenge you to think like a physician. Work through differential diagnosis, history taking, physical exam, and management step-by-step.

📋 200+ Clinical Cases

From common presentations to complex diagnoses across all major medical specialties.

🤖 AI Patient Interviews

Practice taking history with realistic AI patients who respond naturally to your questions.

📊 5-Stage Workflow

Initial differential → History → Physical → Revised differential → Workup & Management.

🎯 4 Training Modes

Learning, Clinical, Quiz, and Small Group modes to match your study style.

Try ReasonDx →
⚙️ Platform 2 of 3

MechanismDx

Build deep understanding of pathophysiology with interactive modules. Learn the "why" behind clinical findings and connect mechanisms to diagnostic reasoning.

📚 35+ Interactive Modules

Covering heart failure, pneumonia, sepsis, electrolytes, acid-base, and more.

🔬 Mechanism-Based Learning

Understand pathophysiology so deeply you can derive the clinical presentation.

📝 500+ Practice Questions

NBME-style MCQs with detailed explanations linked to mechanisms.

🔄 Spaced Repetition

Intelligent review scheduling to maximize long-term retention.

💬 Platform 3 of 3

CoachDx

Learn from an AI attending physician who uses Socratic questioning to develop your clinical reasoning. Think out loud and get guided feedback—not just answers.

🎓 Socratic Method

Your AI coach asks probing questions rather than giving direct answers.

💬 Natural Conversations

Discuss cases the way you would with an attending on rounds.

🧠 Reasoning Development

Build the thought processes that define great clinicians.

🔗 Cross-Platform Links
