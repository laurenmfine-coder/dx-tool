Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from UWorld or Anki?

UWorld tests recall; we teach the reasoning process. Our cases build hypothesis-driven thinking and identify your cognitive blind spots through reasoning pathway fingerprinting. Use our Anki export to complement your existing deck.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No long-term contracts. Cancel from your account settings anytime and keep access through the end of your billing period.

What's the Beta Tester program?

Beta testers get full Pro access at 50% off in exchange for completing a brief monthly feedback survey (~5 min). You help shape the platform and lock in discounted pricing. Requires a .edu email.

How does the referral program work?

After signing up, you get a unique referral code. When a friend joins Pro or Beta with your code, you both get a free month. Refer 3 friends for a free semester, 5 for a free year. Credits are applied automatically to your Stripe subscription.

Is this good for Step 1 prep?

Yes. 138 topics are tagged Step 1-relevant, covering the pathophysiology, embryology, and mechanisms that Step 1 tests. Our approach builds the understanding behind the answer, not just pattern recognition.

What about shelf exams and rotations?

We have dedicated tracks for all 7 core shelf exams (IM, Surgery, Pediatrics, OB/GYN, Psychiatry, Neurology, Family Medicine). Cases are organized by rotation so you can study what you're seeing in clinic.
