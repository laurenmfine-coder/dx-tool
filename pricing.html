Common questions
Is individual access really free forever? ▾
Yes — all 394 cases, all professions, all features listed under Individual. No credit card, no trial period, no paywall. Students can use ReasonDx completely independently of whether their institution has a license.
What does institutional licensing give a program that students can't get for free? ▾
Everything institutional is about the program, not the individual student. Faculty dashboards, cohort analytics, ACGME/LCME exports, LMS integration, custom case authoring, and student roster management — none of these exist in free individual access. A student sees their own data. An institution sees patterns across 200 students and brings that evidence to an accreditation site visit.
How is institutional pricing structured? ▾
Per-student annual licensing with volume tiers. Pricing scales with cohort size and modules included. Contact us for a custom quote — we'll scope based on your program type, size, and specific accreditation needs.
Can we pilot before committing? ▾
Yes. We offer a structured pilot — typically one course block with one cohort — before full rollout. The pilot includes full faculty dashboard access so you can evaluate the analytics before licensing.
How long does implementation take? ▾
Typically 1–3 business days for SSO configuration and LMS setup, followed by a live faculty onboarding session. Most programs are fully deployed within one week of signing.
What accreditation frameworks are supported? ▾
LCME (ED-6), ACGME milestones (all specialties), ARC-PA standards, AANP core competencies, and CCNE. Additional frameworks available on request. All exports are formatted for direct use in site visit documentation.
Is student data FERPA-compliant? ▾
Yes. Institutional accounts include a signed BAA/DPA, FERPA-compliant data handling, and student data is never used for any purpose outside the contracted institution's dashboard.
