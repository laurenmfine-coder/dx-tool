Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Premium before paying?

Yes! Every Premium subscription starts with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. Cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer student discounts?

Our pricing is already designed to be affordable for students. The annual plan offers 33% savings. We also offer institutional licenses for medical schools.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure payment processor, Stripe.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your data is stored securely and we never share it with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
