1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, training level, institution (optional), specialty interest (optional), and expected graduation year (optional).

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the Platform, including pages visited, cases completed, quiz scores, time spent on activities, and feature usage. This data is used to personalize your learning experience and improve the Platform.

Payment Information: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and improve the Platform; personalize your learning experience; track your progress and generate analytics; communicate with you about your account, updates, and educational content; process payments for paid subscriptions; and conduct educational research (with your consent, if applicable).

3. Educational Research

ReasonDx may be used in educational research studies. If your institution participates in a research study, your usage data may be included in anonymized, aggregate analyses. Participation in research is voluntary and governed by the relevant Institutional Review Board (IRB) protocols. You will be informed and asked for consent before any individually identifiable data is used for research purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with: service providers (such as Supabase for authentication, Stripe for payments, and Resend for email delivery) who process data on our behalf; your institution, if you registered through an institutional program (limited to aggregate, anonymized usage data unless otherwise consented); and law enforcement, if required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. Authentication data is managed through Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate information; request deletion of your account and associated data; export your learning progress data; and opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at reasondxeducation@gmail.com.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

ReasonDx uses browser local storage to maintain your session, save preferences, and track learning progress locally. We use Google Analytics to understand Platform usage patterns. You can disable cookies and local storage through your browser settings, though this may affect Platform functionality.

8. Third-Party Services

The Platform integrates with third-party services including Supabase (authentication and database), Stripe (payment processing), Resend (transactional email), Google Analytics (usage analytics), and Flodesk (email newsletter, optional). Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

9. Children's Privacy

ReasonDx is designed for medical students and healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or on-site notification. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at reasondxeducation@gmail.com.
