How CoachDx Works
Socratic coaching, not spoon-feeding
1
You tell CoachDx what you want to work on
A topic, a case, a concept, a question you got wrong. Free text — no templates required.
2
CoachDx asks questions instead of giving answers
It probes your reasoning, pushes back on vague answers, and asks follow-up questions until you can fully defend your thinking.
3
You leave with stronger reasoning, not just the right answer
Sessions are tracked. CoachDx identifies your reasoning patterns and adjusts future sessions to your gaps.
