The SBAR Framework

S

Situation

What is happening right now? Patient identification and immediate concern.

B

Background

What is the clinical context? Relevant history and what led to this.

A

Assessment

What do you think is going on? Your clinical impression and reasoning.

R

Recommendation

What do you need? Specific request or suggested next steps.
