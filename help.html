🎯 About ReasonDx

ReasonDx is a comprehensive clinical reasoning education platform designed for medical students, residents, and healthcare educators. It uses evidence-based pedagogical methods — including spaced repetition, case-based learning, and reasoning pathway analysis — to build durable clinical reasoning skills.

The platform integrates interactive case studies, pathophysiology modules, board-preparation questions, and real-time analytics to create a personalized learning experience that adapts to your strengths and identifies areas for growth.

📖 How to Use ReasonDx

Getting Started

Start from the home page and explore any section that matches your learning goals. There's no required sequence — jump into topics you're studying, try a clinical case, or start with board prep questions.

Your Learning Dashboard

Your Learning Dashboard is your personal command center. It tracks everything you've explored, shows your mastery levels across topics, and uses spaced repetition to bring back content at the optimal time for long-term retention. Look for the 📊 icon in the navigation to access it from any page.

Content Mastery Fingerprint

ReasonDx builds a unique Content Mastery Fingerprint for you as you work through the platform. This visual map shows which reasoning pathways you've developed, where your clinical strengths lie, and what knowledge gaps remain. Access it through your Learning Dashboard or the Analytics Dashboard.

Recommended Learning Paths

  • Pre-clinical students: Start with Topics → explore linked Modules → test with Board Questions
  • Clinical rotations: Jump into Cases and CRT (Clinical Reasoning Trainers) for your current rotation
  • Board prep: Use the Board Prep section with spaced repetition, then review weak areas via Modules
  • Pre-med students: Explore the dedicated Pre-Med section for foundational clinical thinking

✨ Key Features

📊 Content Mastery Fingerprint

As you work through cases, modules, and board questions, ReasonDx builds a visual "fingerprint" of your clinical reasoning. This shows which reasoning pathways (diagnostic, therapeutic, mechanistic) you've developed and which need more practice. It's like a heat map of your medical knowledge.

🔄 Spaced Repetition

Board prep questions and key concepts use an evidence-based spaced repetition algorithm. When you answer a question, the system schedules it for review at the optimal interval — shorter if you got it wrong, longer if you're mastering it. This is proven to improve long-term retention.

🔗 Cross-Linked Content

Every topic, module, case, and question is cross-linked. When you're studying pneumonia, you can instantly jump to the pathophysiology module, try a clinical case, review board questions, or read a deep dive — all on the same topic.

🌓 Dark Mode

Click the 🌓 button in the top-right corner of any page to toggle dark mode for comfortable studying at any time of day.

🏥 Clinical Reasoning Trainers (CRTs)

CRTs present you with a clinical scenario and guide you through the diagnostic reasoning process step by step — gathering history, ordering tests, interpreting results, and arriving at a diagnosis. They teach you how to think, not just what to know.
