📋 Eosinophilia Differential Diagnosis Framework
🦠 REACTIVE (Secondary)
• Parasitic infections (helminths)
• Allergic diseases (asthma, rhinitis, eczema)
• Drug reactions (DRESS, simple drug eosinophilia)
• Adrenal insufficiency
• Solid tumors (paraneoplastic)
🔬 CLONAL (Primary)
• Myeloid neoplasms with eosinophilia
• FIP1L1-PDGFRA fusion (responds to imatinib)
• Chronic eosinophilic leukemia
• Mastocytosis with eosinophilia
• AML, CML with eosinophilia
🔥 INFLAMMATORY
• EGPA (Churg-Strauss)
• Eosinophilic GI diseases (EoE, EG)
• Inflammatory bowel disease
• Sarcoidosis
• IgG4-related disease
❓ IDIOPATHIC HES
• AEC >1,500 × 6 months
• End-organ damage present
• No identifiable cause
• Diagnosis of exclusion
• May require treatment
