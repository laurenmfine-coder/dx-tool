🎯 Key Takeaways
  • BUN:Cr >20:1: Suggests prerenal AKI
  • FENa unreliable with diuretics: Use FEUrea instead
  • UNa unreliable with diuretics: Will be high even in prerenal
  • Clinical context matters: Physical exam + history often more reliable than labs
  • Over-diuresis is common: Balance CHF management with renal perfusion
