MECHANISM
Pathophysiology

The anterior pituitary produces GH, PRL, ACTH, TSH, FSH, LH. The posterior pituitary stores/releases ADH and oxytocin (produced in hypothalamus). Pituitary adenomas are the most common pituitary pathology — classified as functioning (hormone-secreting) or nonfunctioning.

🔑 Mass Effect: Pituitary adenomas >1cm (macroadenomas) compress the optic chiasm → bitemporal hemianopia (classic visual field defect). They can also compress normal pituitary tissue → hypopituitarism.

Pituitary Adenomas by Type

TypeFrequencyClinical Features
ProlactinomaMost common (40%)Women: amenorrhea, galactorrhea. Men: decreased libido, gynecomastia. Dx: PRL >200 = prolactinoma. Tx: dopamine agonist (cabergoline) — medical, not surgical
GH-secreting~20%Acromegaly (adults): enlarged hands/feet, coarsened features, DM, carpal tunnel. Gigantism (children: before epiphyseal closure)
ACTH-secreting~10-15%Cushing disease: central obesity, moon facies, striae, buffalo hump, hyperglycemia, hypertension, osteoporosis
Nonfunctioning~25%Mass effect only: visual field defects, headache, hypopituitarism
⚠️ Pituitary Apoplexy: Sudden hemorrhage into a pituitary adenoma → severe headache, visual loss, ophthalmoplegia, and hemodynamic instability (acute adrenal insufficiency). Emergency: IV steroids + neurosurgical decompression.

🔑 Sheehan Syndrome: Postpartum pituitary necrosis from hemorrhagic shock during delivery. The pituitary enlarges during pregnancy (lactotroph hyperplasia) and is vulnerable to ischemia. Presents with failure to lactate (↓PRL), fatigue (↓TSH, ↓ACTH), amenorrhea (↓LH/FSH).
TREATMENT
Management

Treatment by Adenoma Type

AdenomaFirst-Line Treatment
ProlactinomaDopamine agonist (cabergoline > bromocriptine). Surgery only if medication fails or visual compromise
GH-secreting (acromegaly)Transsphenoidal surgery. Adjuncts: somatostatin analogs (octreotide), GH receptor antagonist (pegvisomant)
ACTH-secreting (Cushing)Transsphenoidal surgery. Adjuncts: ketoconazole, metyrapone, pasireotide
NonfunctioningSurgery if visual compromise or growth. Observation if small and asymptomatic
⚠️ Prolactinoma Exception: Prolactinomas are the ONLY pituitary adenoma where medical therapy is first-line. Do not refer for surgery initially — dopamine agonists shrink the tumor in >80% of cases.
