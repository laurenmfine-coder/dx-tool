Essential formulas for clinical reasoning

Creatinine Clearance (Cockcroft-Gault)

Estimates CrCl for drug dosing adjustments

CrCl = [(140 - age) × weight] / (72 × Cr) × 0.85 if female

Fractional Excretion of Sodium (FENa)

Differentiates pre-renal from intrinsic AKI

FENa = (UNa × PCr) / (PNa × UCr) × 100

Anion Gap

Evaluate metabolic acidosis

AG = Na - (Cl + HCO3) | Normal: 8-12

Delta-Delta Ratio

Identify mixed acid-base disorders in HAGMA

Δ/Δ = (AG - 12) / (24 - HCO3)

Expected pCO2 = 1.5 × HCO3 + 8 (±2)

Corrected Calcium

Adjusts total calcium for albumin level

Corrected Ca = Measured Ca + 0.8 × (4 - Albumin)

Corrected Sodium (for Hyperglycemia)

True sodium in setting of elevated glucose

Corrected Na = Measured Na + 1.6 × [(Glucose - 100) / 100]

mEq/L

Free Water Deficit

Calculate replacement for hypernatremia

Deficit = TBW × [(Na/140) - 1] | TBW = weight × 0.6 (male) or 0.5 (female)

Transtubular Potassium Gradient (TTKG)

Assesses renal K handling in hyper/hypokalemia

TTKG = (UK / PK) / (UOsm / POsm)
