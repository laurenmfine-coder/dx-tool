🎮 What are Adventure Cases?

Unlike traditional learning, Adventure Cases put you in the driver's seat of realistic clinical scenarios.

📖

Story-Driven

Immersive narratives that unfold based on your choices

🔀

Branching Paths

Multiple outcomes depending on your clinical decisions

⏱️

Time Pressure

Feel the urgency of real clinical decision-making

💔

Real Consequences

Your choices affect patient outcomes - for better or worse

🎯 Recommended Learning Path

For the best learning experience, we recommend a two-step approach:

📚

Step 1: Deep Dive

Understand the pathophysiology - learn why things happen

→
🎮

Step 2: Adventure

Apply your knowledge under pressure in realistic scenarios

💡 Pro Tip: Complete a Deep Dive first to understand the underlying mechanisms, then test yourself with the corresponding Adventure Case to practice applying that knowledge in a time-pressured, branching scenario where your decisions affect patient outcomes.
