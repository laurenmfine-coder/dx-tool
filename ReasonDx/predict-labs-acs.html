🎯 Key Takeaways
  • Troponin timing: Takes 2-4 hours to rise — don't rule out MI on one negative
  • Serial markers: Always repeat troponin at 3-6 hours
  • BNP in ACS: Prognostic marker for LV dysfunction
  • Stress hyperglycemia: Common and associated with worse outcomes
  • Monitor K+: Keep 4.0-5.0 to reduce arrhythmia risk
