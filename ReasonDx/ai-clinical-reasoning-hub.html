📚 Lab Prediction Builds Expert Intuition

In allergy/immunology, lab interpretation is nuanced. Committing to predictions before seeing results helps you:

  • Master tryptase timing — When to draw, how to interpret acute vs baseline
  • Interpret component testing — Ara h 2 vs Ara h 8 tells very different stories
  • Navigate immunodeficiency workup — Quantitative Igs are just the start
  • Recognize patterns — T2-high biomarkers, complement cascade, eosinophilia DDx
  • Avoid overtesting — CSU doesn't need extensive allergy panels!
