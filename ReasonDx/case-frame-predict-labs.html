Key Takeaways
  • D-dimer: High sensitivity, low specificity. Use to rule OUT PE.
  • Troponin in PE: Indicates RV strain, not MI. Prognostic marker.
  • ABG in acute PE: Usually respiratory alkalosis with hypoxemia.
