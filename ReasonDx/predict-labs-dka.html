Endocrine Emergency

🎯 Key Takeaways
  • DKA triad: Hyperglycemia + Ketosis + Acidosis
  • Serum K+ lies: Always assume total body K+ depletion
  • AG tracks severity: Higher AG = more ketoacids
  • Bicarb severity: <10 = severe, 10-15 = moderate, 15-18 = mild
  • Monitor closely: K+ falls rapidly with insulin
