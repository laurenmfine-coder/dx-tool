💡 Why Predict Labs?

Expert clinicians don't just order labs—they predict results before seeing them. This mental exercise:

  • Builds pathophysiology intuition - connecting clinical findings to expected lab patterns
  • Improves calibration - teaching you when to trust your clinical judgment
  • Catches surprises - unexpected results become red flags that prompt reconsideration
  • Deepens learning - you remember better when you commit to a prediction first
