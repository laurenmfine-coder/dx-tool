🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why mark borders?
Track progression vs improvement. Erythema may worsen initially despite appropriate antibiotics (dying bacteria release inflammatory mediators).
Why not MRSA coverage for simple cellulitis?
Purulent (abscess) = cover MRSA. Non-purulent cellulitis = usually strep, beta-lactam sufficient.
Why elevate extremity?
Reduces edema, improves lymphatic drainage, speeds healing. Simple but often forgotten.
