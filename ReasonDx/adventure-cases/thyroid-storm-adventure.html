🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why beta-blockers first?
Control life-threatening sympathetic symptoms (tachycardia, fever, agitation). Propranolol also blocks T4→T3 conversion.
Why PTU over methimazole in storm?
PTU blocks peripheral T4→T3 conversion in addition to synthesis inhibition. Give PTU first, then iodine.
Why iodine AFTER thionamide?
Give PTU/methimazole 1 hour before iodine. Otherwise, iodine load can worsen thyrotoxicosis (Jod-Basedow effect).
