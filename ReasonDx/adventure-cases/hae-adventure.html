🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why don't antihistamines work?
HAE is bradykinin-mediated, not histamine. Need C1-INH or kallikrein inhibitor.
Why on-demand AND prophylaxis?
Acute needs immediate treatment. Frequent attacks need prevention.
Why avoid estrogen?
Estrogen increases bradykinin, decreases C1-INH. Can trigger attacks.
