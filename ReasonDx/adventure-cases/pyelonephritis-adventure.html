🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why imaging for complicated pyelo?
CT to rule out abscess, obstruction, emphysematous changes. Indicated if no improvement in 48-72h or severe sepsis.
Why fluoroquinolone caution?
Rising resistance, FDA warnings (tendon, nerve, aortic). Use only if culture-proven susceptible or beta-lactam contraindicated.
Why 7-14 day course?
Shorter courses (5-7d) okay for uncomplicated pyelo with fluoroquinolones. Longer if bacteremia or slow response.
