🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why fidaxomicin over vancomycin?
Lower recurrence rates with fidaxomicin. First-line for initial and recurrent episodes. Vancomycin acceptable alternative.
Why stop inciting antibiotics?
Ongoing antibiotics perpetuate dysbiosis. Stop if possible or narrow spectrum. This is therapeutic.
Why bezlotoxumab for recurrence?
Monoclonal antibody against toxin B. Reduces recurrence by 40%. Consider after 2+ episodes.
