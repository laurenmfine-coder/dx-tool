🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why HR and BP control immediately?
Reduce aortic wall stress (dP/dt). Target HR <60, SBP 100-120. Beta-blocker BEFORE vasodilator to prevent reflex tachycardia.
Why CTA over echo?
CTA is gold standard - shows extent, branch involvement, entry/exit tears. TEE useful if CTA unavailable or for intraop guidance.
Why Type A = emergency surgery?
Ascending aorta involvement risks rupture, tamponade, coronary/cerebral malperfusion. Mortality increases 1-2% per hour without surgery.
