🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why categorize by mechanism?
Treatment differs: distributive (sepsis) = fluids + pressors. Cardiogenic = inotropes, avoid excess fluid. Obstructive = fix obstruction.
Why bedside echo?
RUSH exam differentiates shock types in minutes. IVC, cardiac function, pericardium, aorta, lung sliding, free fluid.
Why lactate trends matter?
Single lactate doesn't differentiate shock types. Clearance with treatment indicates adequate resuscitation.
