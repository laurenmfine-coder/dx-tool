🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why epinephrine first-line?
Reverses ALL effects: alpha-1→vasoconstriction, beta-1→cardiac output, beta-2→bronchodilation + mast cell stabilization.
Why IM over IV?
IM (1:1000, 0.3-0.5mg) provides rapid absorption with lower arrhythmia risk.
Why lateral thigh?
Vastus lateralis has excellent blood flow. Can inject through clothing.
