🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why 15-15-15 rule?
15g fast carbs, recheck in 15 min, repeat if <70. Prevents overcorrection rebound. Glucose tabs preferred.
Why glucagon if can't take PO?
IM/SC glucagon mobilizes hepatic glycogen. Works in 10-15 min. New nasal/auto-injector forms easier.
Why investigate cause?
Recurrent hypoglycemia in non-diabetics = insulinoma, adrenal insufficiency, liver failure. Whipple's triad + workup.
