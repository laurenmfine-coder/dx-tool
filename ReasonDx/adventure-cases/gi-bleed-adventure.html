🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why restrictive transfusion (Hgb <7)?
Liberal shows no benefit. Over-transfusion may increase rebleeding.
Why high-dose PPI?
Acid inhibits platelet aggregation. High pH promotes hemostasis.
Why pre-endoscopy PPI?
Reduces high-risk stigmata, may reduce need for intervention.
