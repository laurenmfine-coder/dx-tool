🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why Wells score before D-dimer?
Pre-test probability determines utility. Low PTP + negative D-dimer safely excludes PE.
Why age-adjusted D-dimer?
D-dimer increases with age. Age×10 cutoff maintains sensitivity, improves specificity.
Why CTPA over V/Q?
Faster, more available, shows alternative diagnoses.
