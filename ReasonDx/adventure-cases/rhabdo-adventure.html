🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why aggressive crystalloid?
Volume expansion dilutes myoglobin, maintains renal perfusion. Goal urine output 200-300 mL/hr initially.
Why monitor potassium closely?
Muscle breakdown releases massive K+. Can cause fatal arrhythmias. Check q4-6h during acute phase.
Why avoid bicarbonate controversy?
Alkalinizing urine may prevent myoglobin precipitation. Evidence weak - focus on volume. Consider if pH <6.5.
