🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why patch testing?
Identifies causative allergen. Type IV hypersensitivity - different from skin prick.
Why 48h and 96h reads?
Some allergens peak at 96-144h. Single read misses delayed reactions.
Why is avoidance critical?
Contact allergy doesn't resolve. Must identify ALL sources of allergen.
