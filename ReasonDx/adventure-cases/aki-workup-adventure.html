🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why urinalysis and sediment?
Muddy brown casts = ATN. RBC casts = glomerulonephritis. WBC casts = pyelonephritis. Bland = prerenal or obstruction.
Why renal ultrasound?
Rules out obstruction (hydronephrosis). Quick, non-invasive, no contrast. Do early in all AKI.
Why check CK in unexplained AKI?
Rhabdomyolysis causes pigment nephropathy. May not have obvious muscle injury. CK >5000 = significant risk.
