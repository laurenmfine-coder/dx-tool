🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why rate control vs rhythm control?
AFFIRM trial showed no mortality difference. Rate control often preferred in older, asymptomatic patients. Rhythm control for symptomatic, younger, or new-onset AFib.
Why anticoagulation based on CHA₂DS₂-VASc?
Score predicts annual stroke risk. Score ≥2 (men) or ≥3 (women) = anticoagulation recommended. Bleeding risk (HAS-BLED) doesn't override stroke prevention.
Why DOACs over warfarin?
Non-inferior efficacy, lower intracranial bleeding, no INR monitoring. Exceptions: mechanical valves, moderate-severe mitral stenosis.
