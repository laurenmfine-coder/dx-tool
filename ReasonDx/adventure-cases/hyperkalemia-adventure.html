🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why calcium first?
Stabilizes cardiac membrane in 1-3 minutes while other treatments work.
Why insulin+glucose together?
Insulin shifts K+ into cells. Glucose prevents hypoglycemia.
Why monitor glucose after?
Hypoglycemia common - check hourly for 4-6h.
