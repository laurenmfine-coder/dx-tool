🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why antibiotics within 1 hour?
Neutropenia = no immune defense. Infection progresses rapidly. Mortality increases with each hour delay.
Why anti-pseudomonal coverage?
Pseudomonas bacteremia in neutropenic patients is rapidly fatal. Empiric cefepime, pip-tazo, or carbapenem.
Why add vancomycin selectively?
Not routine - only if hemodynamically unstable, line infection suspected, MRSA colonization, or severe mucositis.
