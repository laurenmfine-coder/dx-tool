🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why treat underlying cause first?
DIC is always secondary. Sepsis, trauma, malignancy, obstetric. Treat the cause - DIC resolves when trigger removed.
Why not just give FFP/platelets?
'Fueling the fire' is outdated concept. Replace if bleeding or need procedures. Don't withhold for fear of worsening.
Why anticoagulation controversial?
May help in thrombotic-predominant DIC (purpura fulminans). Avoid if overt bleeding. Individualize decision.
