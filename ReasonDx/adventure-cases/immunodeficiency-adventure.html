🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why check quantitative Igs?
IgG <400 with infections suggests CVID. Most common symptomatic PID in adults.
Why vaccine responses?
Functional assessment needed. Check titers to protein and polysaccharide antigens.
Why IgG replacement?
Restores protective antibodies. Reduces infections 70-90%.
