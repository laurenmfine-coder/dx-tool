🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why stress-dose steroids immediately?
Adrenal crisis is life-threatening. Don't wait for cortisol results. Hydrocortisone 100mg IV then 50mg q6-8h.
Why hydrocortisone over dexamethasone?
Hydrocortisone has mineralocorticoid activity. Dexamethasone only if planning stim test (doesn't interfere with cortisol assay).
Why aggressive fluid resuscitation?
Mineralocorticoid deficiency causes severe hypovolemia. NS boluses - often need 2-3L in first hours.
