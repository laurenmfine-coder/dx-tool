Why distinguish histaminergic vs bradykinin?
Different treatments. Histaminergic responds to antihistamines. Bradykinin does NOT.
Why check C4?
Low during AND between attacks in HAE types 1 and 2.
Why stop ACE inhibitors?
ACEi-induced angioedema is bradykinin-mediated. Switch to ARB.
