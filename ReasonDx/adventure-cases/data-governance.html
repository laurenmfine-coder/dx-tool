Your Data, Your Control

Who owns your data? You do (individual accounts) or your institution (institutional licenses).

Can you delete it? Yes, with one click.

🎓 FERPA Compliance

ReasonDx is designed to comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

For Institutions

  • We sign Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with institutional partners
  • We act as a "school official" under FERPA's school official exception
  • We maintain audit logs for all data access
  • We support institutional data retention policies

🔐 Data Security

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Secure login with optional institutional SSO
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with audit logging
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on SOC 2 compliant cloud infrastructure
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups with geographic redundancy

✅ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download your data in CSV, JSON, or PDF format
  • Correct: Update any inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Restrict: Opt out of aggregate analytics
  • Portability: Transfer your learning history to another platform
