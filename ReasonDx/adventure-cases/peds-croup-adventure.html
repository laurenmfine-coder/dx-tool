🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why single-dose dexamethasone?
Reduces airway edema. One dose (0.6 mg/kg) as effective as multiple doses. Works for all severity levels.
Why nebulized epinephrine for severe?
Alpha-adrenergic vasoconstriction reduces mucosal edema. Effect lasts 2 hours - observe for rebound.
Why steeple sign isn't diagnostic?
Classic radiograph finding but low sensitivity. Clinical diagnosis - don't delay treatment for X-ray.
