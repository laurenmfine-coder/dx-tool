🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why no routine LP for simple febrile seizure?
Simple = generalized, <15 min, once in 24h, normal postictal. Low meningitis risk in vaccinated children with clear source.
Why no chronic AEDs?
Simple febrile seizures don't increase epilepsy risk. AED side effects outweigh benefits for benign condition.
Why reassure parents?
Terrifying to witness but excellent prognosis. 1/3 recur but no long-term neurologic sequelae from simple febrile seizures.
