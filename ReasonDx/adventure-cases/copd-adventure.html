🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why bronchodilators over steroids first?
COPD is primarily airflow obstruction, not eosinophilic inflammation. LAMA +/- LABA foundation. Add ICS only if eos ≥300 or frequent exacerbations.
Why oxygen target 88-92%?
COPD patients may have hypercapnic respiratory drive. High-flow O2 can suppress drive → CO2 retention → narcosis.
Why NIV for COPD exacerbation?
Reduces work of breathing, improves ventilation, avoids intubation. Start early - pH <7.35 with hypercapnia = indication.
