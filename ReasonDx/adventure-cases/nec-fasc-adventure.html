🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why surgery within hours?
Source control is definitive treatment. Antibiotics alone are insufficient. Every hour delay increases mortality.
Why broad-spectrum antibiotics?
Polymicrobial (Type I) vs monomicrobial (Type II - GAS). Cover gram-pos, gram-neg, anaerobes until cultures return.
Why LRINEC score caution?
Useful but imperfect. High score suggests nec fasc, but low score doesn't rule out. Clinical suspicion overrides.
