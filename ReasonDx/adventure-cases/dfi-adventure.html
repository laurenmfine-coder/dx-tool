🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why probe-to-bone test?
Sterile metal probe reaching bone = high probability osteomyelitis. Simple, immediate bedside diagnosis.
Why MRI for suspected osteo?
MRI most sensitive/specific for osteomyelitis. X-ray changes lag 2-3 weeks behind infection.
Why vascular assessment?
Ischemia prevents healing and antibiotic delivery. ABI, toe pressures, or angiography. May need revascularization.
