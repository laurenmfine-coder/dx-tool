🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why GDMT quadruple therapy?
ACEi/ARNi + beta-blocker + MRA + SGLT2i each independently reduce mortality. Combined effect is synergistic - start all four, don't wait.
Why ARNi over ACEi?
PARADIGM-HF showed 20% mortality reduction with sacubitril/valsartan vs enalapril. First-line for HFrEF if tolerated.
Why SGLT2i even without diabetes?
DAPA-HF and EMPEROR-Reduced showed benefit regardless of diabetes status. Reduces hospitalizations and CV death.
