🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why check tryptase?
Baseline >20 suggests mastocytosis. Rise during symptoms suggests activation.
Why bone marrow biopsy?
Gold standard. Look for mast cell aggregates, CD25+, KIT mutation.
Why lifelong epinephrine?
Higher anaphylaxis risk. Reactions can be spontaneous or triggered.
