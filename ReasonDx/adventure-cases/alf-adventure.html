🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why N-acetylcysteine even for non-APAP?
NAC improves transplant-free survival even in non-acetaminophen ALF. Mechanism unclear but beneficial.
Why early transplant center transfer?
King's College criteria help predict who needs transplant. Window is narrow - transfer before too sick to survive surgery.
Why monitor for cerebral edema?
Hepatic encephalopathy → ammonia → cerebral edema. ICP monitoring controversial but head of bed elevation, avoid hyperthermia.
