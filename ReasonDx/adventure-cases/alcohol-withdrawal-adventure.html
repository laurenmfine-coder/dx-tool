🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why CIWA protocol?
Symptom-triggered dosing reduces total benzo dose and ICU time vs fixed schedules. Requires trained monitoring.
Why benzodiazepines first-line?
Cross-tolerant with alcohol at GABA receptor. Long-acting (diazepam, chlordiazepoxide) preferred for smoother taper.
Why thiamine before glucose?
Glucose metabolism consumes thiamine. Giving glucose first can precipitate Wernicke encephalopathy. Always thiamine first.
