🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why benzodiazepines first?
GABA-A agonists work fastest. IV lorazepam preferred (longer duration). IM midazolam if no IV access.
Why escalate quickly?
Seizure >5 min unlikely to stop spontaneously. Longer duration = more refractory. Time is brain.
Why second-line agents after benzos?
Benzos alone often insufficient. Fosphenytoin, valproate, or levetiracetam. Don't wait - give immediately after first benzo.
