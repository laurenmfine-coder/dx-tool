🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why CT first, then LP if negative?
CT detects >95% SAH within 6 hours. LP needed if CT negative but clinical suspicion high - look for xanthochromia.
Why nimodipine?
Reduces vasospasm-related delayed cerebral ischemia. Doesn't prevent angiographic vasospasm but improves outcomes.
Why secure aneurysm early?
Rebleeding risk highest in first 24 hours (4-14%). Early coiling or clipping prevents catastrophic rebleed.
