🔬 WHY Chain: Understanding the Mechanism
Why check volume status first?
Hypovolemic (give NS), euvolemic (SIADH - restrict), hypervolemic (heart/liver failure - restrict + diuretics). Treatment differs completely.
Why correct slowly?
Rapid correction → osmotic demyelination syndrome (ODS). Max 8-10 mEq/L in 24h. Slower if chronic or high-risk.
Why hypertonic saline only for severe symptoms?
Seizures, coma, respiratory distress = give 3% saline. Asymptomatic = no emergency, correct slowly.
