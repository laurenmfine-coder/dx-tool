For Researchers: Population-Level Reasoning Data

ReasonLab generates what no manually collected dataset can match: large-sample, naturalistic reasoning process data on real diagnostic cases. When the same case is solved by medical students, residents, and attendings, the resulting pathway data supports studies on diagnostic error patterns, cognitive bias prevalence, expert-novice reasoning differences, and the longitudinal development of diagnostic reasoning skill. Interested in partnering? Contact us about institutional data access.
