🎮 Scenario

A 7-year-old at a birthday party develops lip swelling, generalized urticaria, wheezing, and throat tightness after eating cake. Known peanut allergy.

Step 1: Recognize Anaphylaxis

Anaphylaxis criteria (any ONE):
1. Skin involvement + respiratory OR hypotension
2. Two or more systems after allergen exposure
3. Hypotension after KNOWN allergen

Step 2: Treatment

🔑 EPINEPHRINE IM (anterolateral thigh) is FIRST-LINE. 0.01 mg/kg (max 0.3mg child). Repeat q5-15min. Position supine.
