How Pre-Health Students Use ReasonDx
A proven three-phase approach that develops reasoning alongside content knowledge.
1️⃣
Learn the mechanism
Start with a MechanismDx module to understand the pathophysiology. Don't just memorize — understand why this happens at the cellular and systems level.
2️⃣
Apply it to a patient
Work through a clinical simulation using the same mechanism. This is how the MCAT tests you — a passage about a patient, then questions that require applying basic science.
3️⃣
Debrief your reasoning
After each case, review where your reasoning diverged from expert patterns. This metacognitive step is what turns practice into improvement.
