AAMC Core EPAs for Entering Residency

13 EPAs

The 13 Entrustable Professional Activities (EPAs) defined by the AAMC represent the core clinical activities a medical student must demonstrate before graduating and entering residency. Entrustment level reflects how much supervision you would need to perform each activity safely.
