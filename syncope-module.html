💫 Syncope

Syncope Classification

Reflex (60%)

  • • Vasovagal: Standing, heat, pain
  • • Situational: Cough, micturition
  • • Carotid sinus: Head turning

Orthostatic (15%)

  • • Volume depletion
  • • Medications
  • • Autonomic dysfunction
SBP drop ≥20 or DBP ≥10 in 3 min

Cardiac (15%)

  • • Arrhythmias: VT, heart block
  • • Structural: AS, HOCM, PE
⚠️ MOST DANGEROUS

🚩 High-Risk Features

History

  • • Exertional syncope
  • • No prodrome (sudden)
  • • Palpitations before event
  • • Syncope while supine/sitting
  • • Family hx sudden cardiac death
  • • Known heart disease

ECG Red Flags

  • • QTc >500ms
  • • Brugada pattern
  • • WPW (delta wave)
  • • Complete heart block
  • • VT
  • • ST changes
